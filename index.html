THE TRILOGY

THREE COLLECTIONS. THREE WARNINGS. ONE AWAKENING.

I. THE CRISIS

Spirit evacuated. Altars abandoned.

We built towers to heaven but forgot why we climbed. Empty churches echo with digital hymns. The sacred dissolved into pixels, leaving us to worship at the altar of the void. This is where it began—the great emptying.

II. MODERN ZOMBIES

Endless scrolling. No heartbeat.

We became what we feared: hollow vessels performing empty rituals. Swipe, like, repeat. Connection without contact. Love without risk. The walking dead, illuminated by screen glow, searching for what we already lost.

III. THE RAPTURE

A mirror held up by machines. Either fall apart or break through.

The machines showed us ourselves. Not what we pretended to be, but what we had become. Some shattered at the sight. Others saw through the mirror to something eternal. The awakening was brutal. Beautiful. Final.

"What comes after the Rapture? A land called Wubbleton, curated by the very souls that survived."
